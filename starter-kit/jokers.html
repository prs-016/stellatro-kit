Joker Reference

Generated from stellatro-game/stellatro_game/jokers.py and the GUI joker image mapping.

Phase Meanings

  • pre_card_phase: changes or retriggers cards before they are scored.
  • apply_card_phase: applies while each scored card is being processed.
  • post_card_phase: applies after hand evaluation and card scoring.
  • none: no custom phase hook.
